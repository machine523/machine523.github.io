About Me

Lujun Hong, born in November 1991 in Jiujiang, Jiangxi, is a postdoctoral researcher and Ph.D. candidate at the Technical University of Denmark. He focuses on unidirectional transmission and non-reciprocal optical intelligent control devices in the microwave/terahertz spectrum under the mentorship of Professors Deng Xiaohua and Shen Linfang. Hong has led four projects, including a National Natural Science Foundation of China, and participated in five national and provincial natural science funds and two Danish Villum Fonden projects. Over the past five years, he has published over 30 SCI papers in top optical journals, with 280+ citations and an h-index of 8.
