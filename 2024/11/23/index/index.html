Personal Profile

Hong Lujun, a native of Jiujiang, Jiangxi, was born in November 1991. He is a postdoctoral researcher at the Technical University of Denmark and a jointly trained Ph.D. candidate at the same university. Under the guidance of nationally recognized talents, Professor Deng Xiaohua and Professor Shen Linfang, he mainly engages in research on unidirectional transmission and non-reciprocal optical intelligent control devices in the microwave/terahertz (THz) spectrum. He has led four funded projects, including one National Natural Science Foundation of China, two Jiangxi Province Natural Science Foundation projects (general and youth), and one Nanchang University Young Talent Innovation Fund project; he has participated in three national and provincial natural science funds and two Danish Villum Fonden projects. In the past five years, he has published over 30 SCI papers in authoritative optical journals such as Nature Communications, IEEE/OPTICA Journal of Lightwave Technology, Optics Letters, Optics Express, and Optical Materials Express, with more than 280 citations and an h-index of 8.
